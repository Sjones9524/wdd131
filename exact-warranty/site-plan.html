Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the website is to explain the positives of working with a warranty expert. The website will highlight the achievements that I have made while being a Warranty Administrator for Ford and GM. It will also give peice of mind to potential clients on working with an offsite warranty administrator.

Audience

This would be any car dealership that is looking to streamline their warranty process while also hitting specific metrics put on them by the manufacturer of their brand.

Dynamic elements

Elements that will be dynamic will be a form. This will have the potential client choose or indicate which Dealer Management System (DMS) they use as well as choose what brand of dealership they are. This will also give a typical "onboarding" time frame depending on the brand and DMS.

Content

Home page

The hardest part of the warranty process is making sure that it qualifies all of the different requirements that each manufacturer needs. Once the technician has completed repairs and returns the work order to the service advisor that is where we take over. We will review the work order, the technican comments, and punch times. We will then verify that all applicable labor operations were used. For those brands that are required additional checks for the work order will be done.

We commit to make sure that each work order is looked at, audited and then submitted within three days of being sent to us. If there are any work orders that do not meet requirements to be submitted we will be in contact with your service department within six business hours.

We also commit to working closely with service and parts. Our goal is to grow with the business and communicate effectively with each department. We know that the key to a quick claims process is communication. Without proper communication things are missed and claims are stuck in review for too long.
