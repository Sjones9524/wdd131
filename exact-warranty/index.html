The hardest part of the warranty process is making sure that it qualifies all of the different requirements that each manufacturer needs. Once the technician has completed repairs and returns the work order to the service advisor that is where we take over. We will review the work order, the technician comments, and punch times. We will then verify that all applicable labor operations were used. For those brands that are required additional checks for the work order will be done.

We commit to make sure that each work order is looked at, audited and then submitted within three days of being sent to us. If there are any work orders that do not meet requirements to be submitted we will be in contact with your service department within six business hours. We also commit to working closely with service and parts. Our goal is to grow with the business and communicate effectively with each department. We know that the key to a quick claims process is communication. Without proper communication things are missed and claims are stuck in review for too long.
